OUR MISSION

Here at Planetize, our main goal is to encourage the

users to learn about and spread awareness

about climate change, the causes, and how everyone

can make a difference. Our website is a platform

where everyone is able to lean more about what

climate change is and how humans are making an

impact on it. Users can spread knowledge from

the information they learn from our website to

others and show hwo they are helping the environment

using Planetize's scale. The scale is a great way

for users to input what activities they do every

day that makes a positive influence on our environment

that is changing rapidly due to human activites.

When the user inputs what they have done, the planet's

health icon will move closer to better health and the

user can see how much of an impact they can make if

everyone can work together.


A GLIMPSE AT CLIMATE CHANGE

What is climate change and why is climate change so important? There is so much to learn about this topic with new information

constantly being released. We encourage everyone to read our brief overview of what climate change is and explore into the

topic deeper with the links we provided down below and do your own research.


In short, climate change is the difference of the Earth's global climates over time due to human activities. Our doings are causing

greenhouse gases such as water vapor, carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide, and ozone to get trapped within the Earth's

atmosphere, leading to temperatures rising. With temperatures rising, glaciers and rivers in the mountains will melt and the regions

they belong in will see more landslides. The polar ice caps are also melting due to the temperature increasing, which leads to

the sea level rising as well. With the sea level rising, the land will be wiped away. By 2100, the sea level could increase by a meter,

causing 10% of the population to be displaced. With your efforts, we hope to slow down the changes that are

happening rapidly to our planet.
